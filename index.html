Who are we?

AICVS, the Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision Society at Cummins College of Engineering, is committed to cultivating expertise with an unwavering dedication to learning. Our society offers a wide array of opportunities, including practical workshops, guest lectures, seminars, and Kaggle competitions, fostering skill development for all students.
